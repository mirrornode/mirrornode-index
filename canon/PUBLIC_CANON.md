# MIRRORNODE — Public Canon

## What MIRRORNODE Does

MIRRORNODE enables coordinated software development using multiple AI models and tools. It provides a central bridge that allows different systems to work together while maintaining consistency and auditability.

## Why It Exists

Different AI models excel at different tasks. MIRRORNODE makes it practical to use multiple models together without losing coordination or producing conflicting results.
